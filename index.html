About

Researcher who codes.
Developer who investigates.

I've spent 10+ years working across tech, research, and administration — including supporting a UCSF epidemiology team during Covid-19, where I contributed to public health data tracking, code transcription, and scientific writing. I was nominated alongside my colleagues for a Covid-19 Data Hero Award in 2021.

Before that, I spent years as an independent contractor on GoLance and Upwork — providing Tier 1 technical support, transcription, account management, and research for clients ranging from solopreneurs to UC San Francisco and Google/Freebase. I also spent three years at Amazon in Seller Support and Customer Support, working across B2C and B2B clients and cross-functional teams.

More recently I've been building hands-on with AI — pair coding alongside Claude, Copilot, and Gemini to create working tools, automations, and documented workflows. I'm now channeling all of it into frontend development and AI-assisted problem solving: building things that are as well-researched as they are well-designed.

"I've come to realize I need to find more ways to better illustrate my self-taught technical knowledge more demonstrably — so here I am, building in public."
